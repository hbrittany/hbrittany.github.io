Owl's Bread

Owl’s Bread is a local eatery that bakes their own bread and only buys local and organic products. It’s down to earth and all natural. Their current logo and website does not match their growing population. As a passion project I wanted to rebrand Owl’s Bread to better reflect the sophisticated and modern new crowd the bakery is attracting. With many hand drawn elements I thought this would best reflect the true atmosphere of the eatery.
