Ottawa Votes

A new simple and easy way for the new generations

Expand

Voter turn out has been at an all time low in Ottawa. Ottawa Votes is an app that will allow all eligible voters to submit their votes in a fast, efficient and secure way right from their phone. The app will allow voters to research candidates and make voting selections prior to election day. Upon election day voters will be reminded to submit their selected votes or to make selections and then submit them. Votes will be submitted with Touch ID finger print reader to confirm the users' identity. Ottawa Votes will be specific to Ottawa and will be available for elections of all levels of government.

LG Wash App

Making that tedious chore a little simpler for you

Expand

LG Wash by LG is an app that enables LG washer and dryer customers to set their appliances from their smart phones. Users are able to manage anything and everything about the load as they usually would from the appliance’s control panel. LG Wash will also send notifications to the user’s phone when their loads are complete and ready to be emptied.
