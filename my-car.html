MyCar.ca

As a designer at eDealer.ca I had the opportunity to redesign the MyCar.ca website. Following their current brand guidelines I designed a site that prioritizes the user. Many call to actions buttons allow the user to easily navigate through the site. My Car has 3 different dealership locations so a main priority for the site was making it easy for the user to locate their nearest location to them.
