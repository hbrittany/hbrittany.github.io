LG Wash App

Making that tedious chore a little simpler for you. LG Wash by LG is an app that enables LG washer and dryer customers to set their appliances from their smart phones. Users are able to manage anything and everything about the load as they usually would from the appliance’s control panel. LG Wash will also send notifications to the user’s phone when their loads are complete and ready to be emptied.
