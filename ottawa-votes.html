Ottawa Votes App

A new simple and easy way for the new generations! Voter turn out has been at an all time low in Ottawa. Ottawa Votes is an app that will allow all eligible voters to submit their votes in a fast, efficient and secure way right from their phone. The app will allow voters to research candidates and make voting selections prior to election day. Upon election day voters will be reminded to submit their selected votes. Votes will be submitted with Touch ID finger print reader to confirm the users' identity.
